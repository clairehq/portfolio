Context

This project is about researching a local company and then design a practical solution to solve the biggest problem founded. Our participant group is MLGB-Esport Club which is a local internet café that serves food and drinks. The purpose of this project is about practicing the design thinking process in real life.

The Problems

After 8 weeks of on site observations, interviewing the staffs, we found that:

  • The staffs working in there were too busy during rush hours (they have to make reservations, check-in/check-out computers and serving food/drinks to the customers) which caused failure to take care of the needs of all customers efficiently and leads to customer dissatisfaction and complaints with their service quality.
  • They also didn't have a system to record customers' basic information so that they can't get the unpaid money back which causes loss of profit.

The Goal

  • Reduce the workload of staffs therefore improve serving efficiency and quality
  • Prevent play and run

Ideation

We brainstormed as many ideas as we can to see how might we solve the problems we found.

  • let the customers pay first?
  • hire more employees to reduce everyone's workload?
  • set an alarm to remind the front desk when customers leave?
  • set up a way to track customer’s info and play records?
  • let the customers serving themselves?

Before we dive into converge ideas, we did some research on the two topics below.

Do customers prefer waiting to be served or do it on their own?

Through the research, we found that more customers would choose to do it by themselves if it saves them more time. Most customers hate waiting too long.

How to properly ask for customer information?

Through the research, we found that the simpler the process, the more customers information can get. It is more natural and effective to obtain customer information by registering an account than filling out a form. It will also be more convenient for the company to manage all the customer information when the scale becomes larger.

Solution

We decided to do an all-in-one service App that proposes to realize customers' self-service and information collection. There are 4 main functions:

  1. Self Check-in/Check-out 🖥
  2. Reservation 📆
  3. Food Order 🍙
  4. Game Matching Room 💬

🖥: After logging in to the App, customers can scan the QR code on the corresponding computer to start the timer. Tap the check out button when finish, and the app will automatically deduct the money from the card saved on the app.

📆: Customers can check seat availability in the app, and reserve it in advance and also pay in advance.

🍙: The app allows customers to order food in the app. The staff will receive the order and send it to their seat.

💬: Customers can join the game matching room to play with other customers in the cafe.
