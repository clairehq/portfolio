Context

This project is about to investigate and research a local company and then design a practical solution to solve the biggest problem founded. Our participant group is MLGB-Esport Club which is an local internet cafeÃÅ that serves food and drinks.

The Problems

After 8 weeks of investigation and interviews, we found that:

  • The staffs were too busy during rush hours (they have to make reservations, check-in/check-out computers and serving food/drinks to the customers) which caused failure to take care of the needs of all customers efficiently and leads to customer dissatisfaction and complaints with their service quality.
  • They also didn't have a system to record customers' basic information so that they can't get the unpaid money back which causes loss of profit.

The Goal

  • Reduce the workload of staffs and improve serving efficiency
  • Record customer information in a proper and comfortable way

Research

Before we dive into our solution, we did some research on the two topics below.

Do customers prefer waiting to be served or do it on their own?

Through the research, we found that more customers would choose to do it by themselves if it saves them more time. Most customers hate waiting too long.

How to properly ask for customer information?

Through the research, we found that the simpler the process, the more customers information can get. It is more natural and effective to obtain customer information by registering an account than filling out a form. It will also be more convenient for the company to manage all the customer information when the scale become larger.
