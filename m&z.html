Work About

M&Z Wealth Management

  • Date: December, 2017

  • My Role: Graphic designer

  • Type: Paid work

  • Tools: Illustrator, Sketch

Context

This is a paid work. My job is to design a company logo and company banner for M&Z wealth management company. M&Z company is a financial company that helps clients with financial advice.

Branding

Because it is an investment and financial management company, the most important value for the company is its credit. The company’s credit determines why customers choose them. So the goal of the branding is to achieve the effect which makes customers feel reliable and secure. It should gain clients' trust when they see the branding so that they feel safe to take out their money.

Sketches

First，I sketched out some different ideas.

Final Outcomes

Logo

I chose one idea and made it into a high fidelity vector logo.

Why choose blue color?

Blue is a color that most people like because of the reliability, credibility and communicativeness it conveys. These meanings fit the branding well. Also, blue expresses the power and officialdom of the organization which gives a feeling of "if you let us manage your money, we will help you earn more".

Banner

Finally, I designed the website banner with company name and slogan.

Follow Up

The founder of the company told me that after using my logo and banner on their website, the number of clients increased a lot and she is really happy and thinks the logo brings a lot of luck to her company.
