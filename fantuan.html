WORK ABOUT

Fantuan Delivery App Revamp

  • Duration: 1 week

  • My Role: sole designer

  • App: app store

  • Tools: figma

Overview

Fantuan Delivery is the largest Asian food delivery platform in North America.

The Research

1. collect user feedback

I started with researching about the user group, the target market, and competitors on the internet.

  • User Group: In the early stage, there wre mainly Chinese people, especially international students, Now they are gradually expanding the user group to Asian food lovers.
  • Competitors: Main competitors includes HungryPanda and Kabu Delivery. Other minor competitors includes Doordash, Uber Eats and Skip the Dishes.
2. user persona

I then searched the app store reviews of Fantuan Delivery app and the competitors' review. Then, I found some users and conducted short interviews with them. Then I synthesized the information I collected into two types of users.

3. pain points

I then identified and summarized their pain points.

  • “ cannot quickly order the restaurants and menu I ordered before ”
  • “ I don't know the delivery time, delivery fee and the approximate price level of the restaurent when browsing ”
  • “ The steps to change the dishes on the checkout page are tedious and repetitive ”
  • " can't save favorite store quickly "

App Redesign

i. redesigned store page with better details

I added the store price range and some keywords on the store main page. It allows users to have a quick store overview without further exploring and discover the price range or the food the store provides is not what they expect.

I also inserted a section under the restaurant preview section to show the approximate delivery time and the approximate delivery fee. In the old version, users have to continue to the checkout page to see the delivery time and delivery fee. The newly added section allows users who want to know the delivery time and delivery fee in advance and save their time.

ii. added order again feature

The order again function allows users to quickly edit and order the same items they ordered before. This function eliminates the boring and repeating steps which users need to do before.

iii. redesigned the experience of add/edit items in cart in the confirm order page

In the old version, users cannot edit items on this page. If they want to add or delete items at this step, they need to go back to their cart to do it. I redesigned the experience by making each item section tappable so that users can make changes to the items. I also added an add items button to allow users to add more items if they want.

Also, instead of typing a note to inform the store to include utensils every time, I added a checkbox for whether to include utensils or not.
